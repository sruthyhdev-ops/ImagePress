# Privacy Policy for ImagePress


ImagePress ("we," "our," or "us") provides the ImagePress: Resize & Compress mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
**No Personal Data Collection:** ImagePress does not require a user account, login, or the collection of any personal identification information (PII). 

### 2. Image Processing (On-Device Only)
All image processing—including compression, resizing, and cropping—is performed **locally on your device**. 
* We **do not** upload your images to any external servers. 
* We **do not** have access to the photos you process within the app.

### 3. Permissions
To function correctly, the app may request the following permissions:
* **Storage/Media Access:** To read images you wish to process and save the output to your gallery.

### 4. Third-Party Services
ImagePress does not use third-party tracking or analytics services. (Note: If you add Ads later, you must update this section).

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
